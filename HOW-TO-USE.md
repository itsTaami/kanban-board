# Kanban Board - How to Use

A simple task management app to organize your work across three stages: **To Do**, **In Progress**, and **Done**.

---

## Getting Started

1. Open the app in your browser at `http://localhost:3000`
2. You'll see three columns: To Do, In Progress, and Done

---

## Creating Tasks

1. Click the **+** button in the **To Do** or **In Progress** column header
2. Fill in the task details:
   - **Title** (required): Name your task
   - **Description** (optional): Add more details
   - **Priority**: Choose Low, Medium, or High
   - **Due Date** (optional): Set a deadline
3. Click **Create Task**

> Note: You cannot add tasks directly to the Done column. Tasks must be moved there from In Progress.

---

## Viewing & Editing Tasks

1. Click on any task card to open it
2. Modify the title, description, priority, or due date
3. Click **Save Changes** to update

---

## Moving Tasks

### Method 1: Drag and Drop
- Click and hold any task card
- Drag it to another column
- Release to drop it in the new location

### Method 2: Action Buttons
- **To Do tasks**: Click the task, then click **Start** to move it to In Progress
- **In Progress tasks**: Click the task, then click **Complete** to move it to Done

---

## Deleting Tasks

1. Click on the task you want to delete
2. Click the **Delete** button (red text at bottom left)
3. A confirmation popup will appear
4. Click **Delete** to confirm, or **Cancel** to go back

---

## Task Priority Colors

Tasks are color-coded by priority on the left border:

| Priority | Color  |
|----------|--------|
| Low      | Blue   |
| Medium   | Amber  |
| High     | Red    |

---

## Due Date Indicators

- **Normal**: Gray badge showing the date
- **Due Soon**: Orange badge (within 2 days)
- **Overdue**: Red pulsing badge with "(Overdue)" text

---

## Keyboard Shortcuts

- **Escape**: Close any open modal or popup

---

## Tips

1. Start by adding all your tasks to the **To Do** column
2. Move tasks to **In Progress** when you begin working on them
3. Mark tasks as **Complete** when finished
4. Use priorities to focus on what's most important
5. Set due dates to track deadlines

---

## Workflow Example

```
1. Add task "Design homepage" to To Do
2. Click on the task and press "Start"
3. Task moves to In Progress
4. Work on the design...
5. Click on the task and press "Complete"
6. Task moves to Done
```

---

Enjoy organizing your tasks!
